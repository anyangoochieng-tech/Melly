Web 4.0

Definition

Web 4.0 is the Intelligent Web which is a future vision where humans and machines interact in a seamless, almost biological way.

It refers to a smart and connected systems eg The Smart Assistant, Personalized Health, Smart Cities.

Key characteristics of Web 4.0:

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI) Driven: The web won't just store data; it will understand it. It uses advanced AI to make complex decisions for you.
  • Ubiquity: The internet is no longer just on your phone or laptop; it is in your glasses, your clothes, your car, and your home (the Internet of Things taken to the extreme).
  • Real-Time Interaction: Interaction happens at the speed of thought, often using voice or even Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCI) like Neuralink.
  • Symbiosis: A collaborative relationship where the "machine" learns your habits so well it can perform tasks before you even ask.
